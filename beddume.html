Beddume

Beddume is what makes Pesce Beddpo not just a regular clownfish.

Character

Before the animated series, and any of his wonderful adventures, Pesce Beddo was just a regular clownfish, not a Beddo Diglie, just a regular clownfish that swam in the sea, until, he found a huge treasure of Beddume, that after 1000+ years of research, no one had found yet.
