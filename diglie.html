Beddo Diglie

A beddo diglie (diglie for short) is a less powerful type of fish in the Beddo Universe.

Character

A Beddo Diglie is a clownfish, that has been filled with less Beddume. It is less powerful and less strong than Pesce Beddo
There are also a lot of Beddo Diglies, unlike, Pesce Beddos, that only one exists.
